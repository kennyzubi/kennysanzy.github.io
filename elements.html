What is
NICIS II?

NICIS II is a comprehensive, digital system introduced by the Nigeria Customs Service to manage all trade-related activities electronically. It serves as a single window platform for handling customs declarations, payments, and communications between traders, customs agents, and other regulatory bodies. Before NICIS II, customs procedures were largely manual, leading to delays, inconsistencies, and potential for corruption.
